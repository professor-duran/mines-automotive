## Overview

Accelerated 8-week online graduate course taught as an independent study. Develops quantitative finance skills in Python, including walk-forward validation, regime detection, and tail hedging. Taught by automotive track faculty; included here because graduates heading into OEM, supplier, and startup automotive roles benefit from fluency in quantitative risk, P&L, and capital-allocation thinking.

## Learning outcomes

- Build and validate quantitative strategies in Python using walk-forward methods.
- Analyze regime shifts and their effect on engineered systems' economic behavior.
- Design and stress-test tail-risk hedges.
- Translate engineering outcomes into financial language for investors and partners.
