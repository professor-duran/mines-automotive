## Overview

Cross-listed course on technology commercialization applied to the automotive sector. Covers intellectual property, startup formation, customer discovery, partnerships with OEMs and suppliers, and the pathway from research output to a launched product in automotive markets.

## Learning outcomes

- Identify commercialization pathways for automotive technologies.
- Conduct structured customer discovery with industry stakeholders.
- Evaluate IP, funding, and partnership options for a venture.
- Develop an investor-ready overview of an automotive technology concept.
